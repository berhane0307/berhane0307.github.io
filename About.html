My Name is Berhane Fesehaye, originally from Eritrea. Having spent four years in neighboring Ethiopia and another four in New Zealand. My academic background lies in accounting, and I served as an accountant in Eritrea for three years. Beyond my professional life, I find joy in singing and playing traditional instruments like the kirar and harp. In Ethiopia, my focus shifted to digital marketing where I also embraced the role of a YouTuber with my own channel. Looking ahead, my aspirations revolve around becoming an educated individual, taking on responsibilities for my family and community, with the ultimate goal of returning to my sacred homeland Eritrea to spend the rest of my life there.
